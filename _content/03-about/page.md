This theme is based off of the baseline <a href="https://github.com/statamicthemes/statarkers-theme">theme</a> put together by Garth Redfern. It uses the <a href="http://getbootstrap.com/">Bootstrap Framework</a>. The theme requires a Statamic license which is purchase-able from their <a href="http://www.statamic.com/">website</a>. Special thanks to Jason Varga for the <a href="https://github.com/pixelfear/Statamic-Globals">global addon</a>. It was designed by <a href="http://stephaniehider.com">Stephanie Hider</a> for a gaming buddy who tinkers, not in the good way, with his [gaming blog](http://www.aldshotfirst.com/) too much. =D
